FAQ

What is Open HRMS Core?

Open HRMS Core is an all-in-one human resource management solution designed to streamline HR operations in your company. It offers a suite of tools for managing employees, leaves, loans, shifts, announcements, and much more.

Can I manage multiple companies with Open HRMS Core?

Yes, Open HRMS Core supports a multi-company environment, allowing you to manage HR operations across different branches or companies under one platform.

Can I keep track of property and assets assigned to employees?

Yes, with the Custody/Property Management feature, you can maintain records of all the company property (laptops, phones, etc.) assigned to employees and track them until the items are returned.

How does the system handle employee transfers between branches?

The Employee Branch Transfer feature allows easy transfer of employees from one branch to another, while maintaining all necessary records and ensuring continuity in employment.

How does the system manage employee vacations?

The Vacation Management feature tracks employee leaves and vacations. Managers can approve or reject leave requests directly from the system, making the process quick and transparent.

Can leave requests be automated?

Yes, the Automation on Leave Request Mails feature automates the leave request process by generating requests directly from incoming emails. This reduces manual entry and speeds up processing.
